## Brand Understanding

### About the Brand
Acme Kitchenware is a premium kitchen tools brand focused on home cooks who value quality and durability. Founded in 2019, they've built a reputation for well-designed, functional products that last.

### Target Customer
Home cooking enthusiasts aged 28-45, primarily female, who cook 4+ times per week. They value quality over price and are willing to pay premium for tools that perform well and last.

### Key Value Propositions
- Premium materials (stainless steel, solid wood)
- Ergonomic designs tested with real home cooks
- 5-year warranty on all products
- Aesthetic designs that look good in modern kitchens

### Client Goals
- Increase revenue 40% YoY
- Expand product line presence in "kitchen organization" category
- Maintain ACOS below 25%
- Achieve 4.5+ rating across all products
