## Quick Reference

### Common Commands to Tell Claude

**Start a new log entry:**
> "I want to log my weekly update for [Brand Name]"

**Onboard a new brand:**
> "Let's onboard a new brand called [Brand Name]"

**Onboard a new product:**
> "Let's onboard product [ASIN] for [Brand Name]"

**Review recent activity:**
> "Show me recent logs for [Brand Name]"

**Query specific info:**
> "What actions did we take on [Brand Name] regarding advertising last month?"

**Create a POA:**
> "I want to log a plan of action for [Brand Name]"
